About BigPlanetarium

We're a Bristol-based planetarium and we love space! We are predominantly an educational organisation, offering entertainment for the public and for schools of all ages.

Our mission is simple: science is for everyone. No jargon, no gatekeeping — just wonder, curiosity, and the breathtaking scale of the universe explained in a way anyone can enjoy.

From stunning dome projections to hands-on exhibits, every visit promises something new to discover.

Our Home Galaxy

The Milky Way is a barred spiral galaxy containing over 200 billion stars — including our own Sun. Stretching roughly 100,000 light-years across, it's the cosmic neighbourhood we call home.

🌌

Size & Scale

The Milky Way spans approximately 100,000 light-years in diameter and is estimated to contain 100–400 billion stars.

🌀

Shape

It's a barred spiral galaxy — our Solar System sits roughly 26,000 light-years from the galactic centre in the Orion Arm.

⏳

Age

The Milky Way is around 13.6 billion years old, making it nearly as old as the universe itself, which is 13.8 billion years.

🕳️

Black Hole

At the galactic centre lies Sagittarius A*, a supermassive black hole about 4 million times the mass of our Sun.

🔆

Our Sun

The Sun completes one orbit around the Milky Way centre — a "cosmic year" — every 225–250 million years.

🌠

Neighbouring Galaxies

The Andromeda Galaxy is our nearest large neighbour, approximately 2.537 million light-years away, and on course to merge with us in ~4.5 billion years.

Designed for Everyone

We believe space is for all. Here's how we make BigPlanetarium accessible for every visitor.

⌨️ Keyboard Navigation

Every interactive element can be reached and activated using a keyboard alone. Planet cards are fully focusable and operable without a mouse.

🖼️ Descriptive Alt Text

All images include meaningful alternative text so screen reader users get the same rich context as sighted visitors.

🎨 Colour Contrast

Text and UI elements meet WCAG AA contrast ratio requirements (4.5:1 minimum), ensuring readability for users with low vision.

📐 Responsive & Scalable

The site works on all screen sizes — from phone to desktop — and supports browser text zoom up to 200% without losing content or function.

🏷️ ARIA Landmarks

Semantic HTML5 landmarks (nav, main, section, footer) and ARIA labels help screen readers understand page structure instantly.

⏭️ Skip Navigation

A visible skip link at the top lets keyboard and screen reader users jump directly to the main content, bypassing the navigation bar.
